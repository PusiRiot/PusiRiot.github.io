— Get to know the game

A Labyrinthic Mansion

Brawlight Manor is a competitive 1v1 game where two players explore a manor in the dark armed with their flashlights.

To win, they must claim crystals scattered around the map. Light does not just reveal the environment: it is also the center of combat, survival, and control of space.

With quick matches, melee combat with parry, unique special abilities, and maze-like maps that feel alive, each confrontation becomes a strategic duel of risk and proximity where getting close to your opponent is as dangerous as necessary.

At a glance

  • Local PvP Multiplayer for 2 Players
  • Total and immersive darkness
  • Dominate the manor by capturing crystals
  • Retro PSX aesthetic
  • Sabotage your opponents
  • Play matches and unlock the lore

— Core aspects

Features

💎

Crystal System

Each room hides crystals that you must find and light on with you flashlight. The more you light on, the more points you earn, however your opponent can take them away from you at any moment.

⚔️

Combat and Sabotage

The light melee, heavy melee, and parry system creates a triangle of decisions based on timing, risk assessment, and rewatd, closer to a simplified fighting game than a chaotic brawler.

🌑

Find Yourself in the Darkness

Dark and labyrinthic corridors that you will have to cross with the help of your flashlight. The crystals light up when you shine on them, but soon darkness prevails again.

👻

The Manor is Haunted

Haunted events will trigger during the game. Doors open and close, revealing new paths to cross.
